About Tyres

How to read tyre size?

The first number from the left denotes the width of the tyre. The second number denotes the height of the sidewall which is also known as aspect ratio. It is expressed as percentage of the width. The alphabet in the 3rd position talks about the tyre construction type. The last number denoted the rim size.

How do I take care of my new tyres?

1. With a tyre-pressure gauge, check your tyre pressure at least once a month. 2. Check your tyres for cuts, snags, punctures, other damages, and uneven tyre wear on a regular basis. 3. Have your alignment examined as soon as you notice abnormal tread wear. 4. When mounting the new tyres on the wheels, double-check that they are balanced. 5. Change your worn out tyres as soon as you notice. Otherwise It may cuse to many other issues of your vehicle and may cause to handling & braking.
